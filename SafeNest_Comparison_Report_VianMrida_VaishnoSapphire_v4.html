Section 8 — About These Projects
In a Nutshell
In a Nutshell Vian Mrida is an ultra-luxury high-rise at ORR Exit No. 2, Kollur — one of Hyderabad's fastest-growing western corridors. It offers 356 spacious 2 and 3BHK homes in a single G+32 tower built using Mivan aluminium formwork on 2.62 acres. What truly sets it apart is a four-level sky clubhouse spanning floors 23–26 — an infinity pool at 280 feet, a squash court, home theatre, and banquet hall across 40,000 sqft of amenity space. With HMDA and RERA approvals in place, 8-ft teak doors, biometric locks, 10-ft corridors, and 100% power backup, Vian Mrida is for buyers who want a landmark address with a lifestyle to match — at a price point that remains competitive for the Kollur SEZ belt. Vaishno Sapphire — marketed as the Gem of Kollur — is a thoughtfully designed residential community of 276 homes across two towers of 12 floors each, located just 2 minutes from the ORR. With spacious 2 and 3BHK homes starting from 1,365 sqft and all-in pricing from approximately ₹1.04 Cr, it offers outstanding value in one of Hyderabad's most active growth corridors. The full-facility clubhouse includes a swimming pool, Salon & SPA, Pickleball court, Amphitheatre, Pet Park, and much more — a rich community lifestyle at a mid-rise scale. TSRERA registered and on track for August 2030, Vaishno Sapphire is a strong choice for families looking for a warm, amenity-rich community in a well-connected part of the city.

Summary — Helping You Decide

What Both Projects Offer

Both Vian Mrida and Vaishno Sapphire are located in Kollur on Hyderabad's western ORR corridor — one of the city's most sought-after growth destinations. Both offer RERA-registered 2 and 3BHK homes, both are at an early but active construction stage, and both are scheduled for completion around 2030. Either project gives you a well-located home in a market that has seen consistent appreciation. The choice between them comes down to lifestyle preference and budget range.

Pricing & Value

Vaishno Sapphire offers a lower entry point — from ₹1.04 Cr for a 1,365 sqft 2BHK at ₹5,999/sqft. Vian Mrida starts at ₹1.13 Cr for a slightly larger 1,395 sqft 2BHK at ₹6,600/sqft. At the upper end, Mrida's largest 3BHK is 2,360 sqft — significantly larger than Sapphire's 2,030 sqft maximum — at a proportionally modest premium. If maximising home size is the priority, Mrida delivers more space per rupee at the upper end.

Lifestyle & Amenities

Vian Mrida makes a statement with its 4-level sky clubhouse — 40,000 sqft of amenity space giving every resident over 112 sqft of dedicated lifestyle area. The infinity pool at 280 feet, home theatre, and Zen Garden are genuinely rare in Hyderabad at this price point. Vaishno Sapphire takes a different approach — a richly programmed ground-level community with a swimming pool, SPA, Amphitheatre, Forest Walk, Pet Park, Skating Rink and Creche. It may be smaller in total area, but it covers almost every lifestyle need a family could have. Both are excellent — it comes down to whether you prefer a vertical landmark or a grounded community.

Community Feel

With 356 homes in a single G+32 tower, Vian Mrida offers the energy and facilities of a large urban development. Vaishno Sapphire's 276 homes across two 12-floor towers create a quieter, more intimate neighbourhood feel. Families who value knowing their neighbours and a relaxed pace often prefer the mid-rise scale. Those who want the amenities and address of a landmark high-rise will lean towards Mrida.
