Section 8 — About the Projects
Project Overview
Project Overview Vian Mrida is an ultra-luxury high-rise at ORR Exit No. 2, Kollur — one of Hyderabad's fastest-growing western corridors — offering 356 apartments across 2BHK and 3BHK configurations in a single G+32 Mivan-constructed tower on 2.62 acres. The standout feature is a four-level sky clubhouse spanning floors 23–26, including an infinity edge pool 280 feet above ground, squash court, home theatre, and banquet hall — all within a 40,000 sqft amenity zone. With HMDA and RERA approvals in place, 8-ft teak doors, biometric locks, 10-ft wide corridors, and 100% DG backup, the project targets buyers seeking landmark-grade lifestyle at competitive pricing in the Kollur SEZ belt. Vaishno Sapphire (marketed as Gem of Kollur) is a low-density residential community of 276 apartments across 2 towers of 12 floors each, located in Kollur — one of Hyderabad's fastest-growing western corridor neighbourhoods, just 2 minutes from the ORR. The project offers spacious 2BHK and 3BHK homes ranging from 1,365 to 2,030 sqft, with a full-facility clubhouse including a swimming pool, Salon & SPA, Banquet Hall, and Pickleball court, all at an all-in price starting from approximately ₹1.04 Cr. With TSRERA registration in place, a construction timeline targeting August 2030, and a developer-declared density of 92 units per acre, this project targets buyers looking for a mid-rise, amenity-rich home in a well-connected Hyderabad growth corridor.

Summary Recommendation

Overview

Both projects sit in the same micro-market — Kollur on Hyderabad's western ORR corridor — and both are at an early construction stage targeting completion around August 2030. The choice comes down to what you value most: landmark lifestyle and scale, or community feel and lower entry cost.

Pricing & Value

Vaishno Sapphire is the more affordable option, starting at approximately ₹1.04 Cr for a 1,365 sqft 2BHK versus Mrida's ₹1.13 Cr for a 1,395 sqft 2BHK. At the upper end, Mrida's 2,360 sqft 3BHK at ₹1.99 Cr offers significantly more space than Sapphire's 2,030 sqft 3BHK at ₹1.59 Cr — a proportionally smaller premium for a meaningfully larger home.

Scale & Lifestyle

Vian Mrida leads on amenities. Its 4-level sky clubhouse — infinity pool at 280 ft, squash court, home theatre — gives 112 sqft of amenity space per resident versus Sapphire's 50 sqft. If landmark lifestyle features matter, Mrida wins. Sapphire compensates with thoughtful outdoor amenities — Pet Park, Skating Rink, Amphitheatre, Forest Walk — that many families value equally.

Density & Community

Mrida is the denser project at 136 units/acre versus Sapphire's 92–102 units/acre. Buyers who prefer a quieter mid-rise community will find Sapphire more comfortable. Buyers who want a high-rise landmark address will prefer Mrida.

Risk Flags

Both are first ventures for their respective developers — no delivery track record to assess. Mrida's handover timeline has a conflict: developer claims 36 months, RERA's legal deadline is January 2030 (44 months). Sapphire's 63-month RERA timeline is longer but internally consistent.
