Section 2 — Why SafeNest Selected Each Project

The case for each — individually

Project 1Prestige VerdureBest for: End-user family
Prestige Verdure was selected because it offers the strongest combination of construction safety and lifestyle quality within your budget. The project is in Finishing Stage — all floors complete, interiors being fitted — meaning the largest risk for any off-plan buyer is already behind this project. Possession in December 2026 is the earliest in this shortlist.

The 112 sft of amenity space per home — against a belt average of 22 sft — is the most significant lifestyle differentiator in this comparison. For a family that will actually live here, this compounds every single day. The 10.6 ft ceilings and 10.5 ft palatial corridors are specifications not found in any other project at this price point in Kollur. Prestige Group's track record of 14 delivered projects provides confidence for an end-user who wants to move in, not speculate. This project is most naturally suited for an IT professional family prioritising construction safety and on-time possession.
Project 2Lansum El PalacioBest for: NRI / HNI buyer
Lansum El Palacio was selected because it offers the highest specification product in this shortlist — a Luxury Index of 91, the only rooftop pool at 380 ft elevation in Kokapet, Italian marble as standard, and a Sky Lounge at G+34. For a buyer who values what they own over when they get it, these features are simply unavailable elsewhere at this price point.

The Kokapet location provides the shortest drive to Financial District — 8 minutes via ORR. One item to note: the JV structure involves a DAGPA arrangement. This is within normal parameters — we recommend your lawyer verify the mortgage release clause in the AOS. This project is most naturally suited for a buyer with patient capital who values specification above the fastest possible possession.
Project 3IRA PanoramaBest for: Budget-conscious end-user
IRA Panorama was selected because it offers the most accessible price point in this shortlist — ₹7,200 /sft on CLP, and the only 50:50 plan available among these three projects at this unit size in Narsingi. For a buyer where upfront outflow matters, this payment structure reduces early financial pressure significantly.

The project's structure is past 50% complete with a March 2027 possession target. The Narsingi location provides solid ORR access (1.1 km) with 18-minute connectivity to Financial District. Product specifications — 9 ft ceilings, RCC construction — are comfortably at market standard rather than above it. This project is most naturally suited for a first-time or budget-conscious end-user who wants a safe, RERA-registered home at the most accessible price in this shortlist.
